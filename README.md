Samuni – Summary
The field shifts. The orbs drift.
Some obey you. Some resist you. All of them watch.
You draw the links.
The forces answer.
Red cuts.
Blue stirs.
Yellow gathers.
Green awakens.
Purple remembers you.
Everything else is interference in the dark.
There is no grid.
No clock.
Only motion, intent, and what you choose to touch.
Left reveals what’s asked of you.
Right reveals who you may become.
Up is the doorway.
Seasons rewrite the star at your center.
Upgrades alter your shape in the chaos.
Cosmetics mask nothing.
The Arena waits for those who need a harder truth.
Skill is the only offering required.
The rest is noise.
